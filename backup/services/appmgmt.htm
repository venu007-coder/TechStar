For organizations with limited IT resources that outsource software maintenance, Altech Star undertakes various levels of responsibilities in application management and also help clients make decisions on revamping their systems.
 

Our well-defined methodology assists in tackling issues like transition planning, incident management and change management including evaluation of application viability, transition planning, system documentation, knowledge transfer, shadow support, and take over. Once service levels are agreed upon, we provide clients with clear benchmarks for performance measurement. Our application maintenance processes include four interlinked activities - corrective maintenance, adaptive maintenance, preventive and perfect maintenance.

Altech's re-engineering and migration services help organizations consolidate activities on fewer platforms and integrate applications more effectively. Our experts assess the application and technology portfolios and internal processes, select technology strategies, conduct tactical and long-range planning, manage change, and conduct knowledge transfer. Re-engineering involves migration across platforms, databases and operating systems. Altech Star uses customized solutions and a tool-based approach to re-engineering and migration projects.

Our methodologies integrate the use of tools to automate re-engineering tasks such as transformation and testing. This approach leads to faster delivery schedules and greater accuracy. Our range of services includes assessment of the system for re-engineering right up to complete integration. We provide support services like documentation, code optimization, code redesign, component replacement.
