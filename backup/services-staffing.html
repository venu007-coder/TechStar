Staffing Services

Altech Star Inc., is a leading provider of IT professionals on a project (temporary) and full-time basis. Our staffing solutions are reliable, flexible and cost effective and we have the experience and expertise to offer the entire range of Staffing Services. We understand every work situation in the market place and suggest the best fit for your needs and requirements.

Altech Star has developed a recruitment process that screens, identifies and sources the best possible candidates in the shortest time frame. It is our ability to identify people with varied and complex skills and deploy them rapidly that differentiates us. We can ramp up to provide larger teams of IT professionals as your requirements.


Our technical consultants are leaders in executing, implementing and maintaining software projects. They assist our clients in all aspects of software development and IT management. Clients get exactly what they need, and more, as our staffing experts work as an extended arm of your recruitment department.


Altech Star’s approach sharpens your competitive edge in the following ways:
 

  • Lowers cost for recruiting and hiring
  • Provides access to specialized recruitment expertise normally not available
  • Reduces time-to-hire
  • Provides better quality hires
