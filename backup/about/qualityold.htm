ltech Star has instituted stringent quality assurance measures following the SEI CMM and ISO 9001 standards. The company’s quality framework documents how software teams should perform and the Quality Assurance Group ensures compliance with quality goals and processes for all services.
 

Altech’s Process and Quality Management practice constitutes experienced process specialists and quality auditors who provide clients with suitable models and processes for achieving consistency, efficiency, and long-term success. Lower cost of ownership, involving systems that avoids cost overruns and are more maintainable
On-time delivery, avoiding the unplanned use of resources
Giving customers what they want, when they want it, at the right price
Meeting service level requirements
