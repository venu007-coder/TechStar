A specialty software that provides digital imaging based on fetal ultrasound images integrated with analysis of fetal growth
OB SCAN is a specialty image and report management solution for Obstetrics and Gynecology providing digital image management of fetal ultrasound images together with integrated fetal growth analysis and dynamic reporting.

OB SCAN provides single frame video capture, digital read, storage and review, and has a Dynamic Report Building Module.

Features
Single click auto installation
Image & data backup onto a CD
Single click power point presentation with images
Multi-page reporting

Benefits
Cost savings on documentation and printing
Powerful processing and annotation tools
I / II / III trimester reporting
Fetal growth analysis and charts
Multiple visits readily seen
Gestational Age (GA) calculation based on CRL, BPD, HC, AC, FL etc..
AUA calculation
Estimation of fetus weight, fetus ratio, AFI
Multiple gestational analysis
Multiple Fetus reporting

Diagnosis reports can be prepared along with the images and possible annotations on the images by the doctors...
