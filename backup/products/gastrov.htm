An image and report management software that was created by experienced technical teams in collaboration with a group of doctors
GastroView is a digital image management of gastro images with patient demographic and diagnostic details for enabling the endoscopists to efficiently handle with record management and report publishing. This would provide simple and user-friendly interface for capturing images from the endoscopes equipment, feeding the patient details for performing diagnosis and analysis.

GastroView provides single frame video capture, storage, query and retrieve, review of multiple visits record and incorporates the flexible template based customizable reporting program along with standard reports specially designed to suite the requirements of the endoscopist's in different examination.

Integrated Utility wizards provide facility to manage users, export patient records to CD ROM, managing endoscopes and adding referring doctor names, procedures, custom comments and findings. Items in the wizards can be added or removed and saved at any time for retrieval of the settings. This provides full motion video capture to record an entire ultrasound procedure as a running video clip and digital image management tools for post acquisition still image processing.

The video-editing suite provides functionality to extract still frames or a particular portion of the video clip from captured lengthy clip, multiple clips can be merged to single clip. The system operates in both standalone and network mode and run on Windows 95, 98, or NT operating systems
