ABOUT US

Altech Star Inc., is a boutique consulting company with core expertise in Healthcare, Information Technology, Staffing Services and Business Process Outsourcing; and is rapidly spreading its operations across the world in all four verticals.

Established in 2002, Altech Star Inc. is a part of ETA Star group, that has revenues in excess of $5 billion, 72000 employees, a global network of 140 entities and offices in 22 countries. ETA Star operates in 16 industry verticals that include Contracting and Engineering, Trading, Shipping, Manufacturing and Assembly, Facility Management, Automobile, Real Estate, Retail, Services, Healthcare, Aviation, Learning and Education, Insurance, Hospitality and Oil and Natural Gas.

For our fast growing list of clients across the globe, who seek world-class solutions in Healthcare, Information Technology, Staffing services and BPO Services, Altech Star Inc., is the one trusted destination today. We have undertaken Consulting projects, Implementation projects and BPO services for clients in the USA, UK and UAE. Our expertise and experience ensures that we deliver to our client’s satisfaction always.

The mantra of Altech Star Inc. is simple, think “global” in terms of quality, delivery and service, and act “local” in terms of accessibility, approachability and response time. We manage this difficult and fine balance because we believe that the building blocks of all human endeavor are people - we put people at the center of everything. And to people, be it employees, stakeholders or customers, we show not just a human face, but our soul as well. We believe that it is the way to build trust, to promote a culture of excellence and transparency, and to create an environment that is creative, vibrant and energetic.

The group’s approach to business and people is epitomized by the wide variety of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities undertaken by ETA Star. It’s CSR activities include procurement and distribution of mobile medicare units, building homes for the homeless, providing aid to earthquake, tsunami and other natural disaster victims and engaging in clean up campaigns and environment related programs.
