Mediview is an image and report management software for Ultrasound, Color Doppler, Video Endoscope, Laparoscope, Uretheroscope, Arthroscope, Sinusscope, Endo Cameras, Image Intensifiers and Electron Microscopes etc. It allows users to capture, store and archive medical reports and customize reports. It is currently being used by over 200 healthcare facilities in India and abroad.

Gastro Expert helps medical practitioners scan and obtain detailed accounts of diagnosed findings through a database of gastro probabilities invoked through an intelligently compiled GUI. It has been extensively accepted by several reputed hospitals across the country.

GastroView is a digital image management system of gastro images with patient demographic and diagnostic details and helps with record management and report publishing. It covers four procedures – Esophago Gastro Duodenoscopy (EGD), Colonoscopy, Endoscopic Retrograde Colongio Pancreatography (ERCP) and Therapeutic Procedure. We have a specialty tool called Medinet Gastroview that provides all the features of Gastroview and additionally includes the option of Multiple Capturing Station.

OB SCAN is a specialty image and report management solution for Obstetrics and Gynecology providing digital image management of fetal ultrasound images together with integrated fetal growth analysis and dynamic reporting. OB SCAN provides single frame video capture, digital read, storage and review, and has a Dynamic Report Building Module.

BizClaire is a fully integrated business solution that provides organizations complete control over planning and management of all operations. It is easy-to-view, provides real-time information, reduces operating costs and is modular. BizClaire has been designed specifically for mid-sized companies. BizClaire modules include Sales, Finance, Purchase, Inventory, Payroll, Material Planning and Production Planning.

FinAC is a comprehensive financial application that manages the financial aspects of an organization with multi-currency functionality. It is a robust accounting package with extensive built-in reporting capabilities. FinAC has extendable accounting solution supporting multi-users on VB & Oracle and provides real time reports, funds flow statements and a budget creation tools. It is well-suited for medium and large corporations helping enterprises keep control on their businesses.

ChartWeaver is a platform-independent, web-based,, 3D chart generating tool that is useful for companies and developers looking for instant chart generation. It sends streams of interactive SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) content to the browser in response to enterprise business data for graphic visualization. Charts may be customized by the GUI based Chart Designer tool or ChartWeaver APIs. ChartWeaver is designed to integrate with any J2EE application. The application may be integrated with any enterprise Java based Web Application.

Value Insight is a Business Performance Management tool that helps businesses monitor and analyze business performance via Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). It delivers information from data sources relating to strategic objectives and sends alerts where there are deviations. Value Insight can be deployed on ERP, CRM, SCM and other legacy applications. The tool is built on our proprietary Hyper Cube OLAP that delivers better performance than conventional OLAP.

Stelar is an integrated Retail Banking System. Its modules include Savings and Checking Accounts, Loans and Deposits, Remittances and Trade Finance. Stelar integrates with back office general ledger, providing an excellent cost effective solution for multi-branch operations. It performs operations that include Customer maintenance, Account maintenance, Teller operations, Check maintenance, Loan processing, Interest automation, ATM functions and Clearing operations.
