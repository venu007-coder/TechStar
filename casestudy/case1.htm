Client: Volkswagen Credit Inc, II

Altech Star was associated with Volkswagen Credit Inc (VCI) for the development and delivery of leasing data tool that enabled VSI save more than USD25 million through forecasting the leased vehicles several years ahead. The team exploited Oracle's OCI technology and coupled this with flexible GUI framework to make VCI realize their data in a new dimension.

The synchronized multithreaded feature of this application enabled smooth functioning of multiple operations simultaneously allowing the user to engage in other tasks, saving time and cost.
