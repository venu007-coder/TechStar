Client: Electrolux-Kelvinator India, Limited

Electrolux-Kelvinator is engaged in the business of manufacturing refrigerators, air-conditioners and washing machines.

Altech Star was associated with Electrolux-Kelvinator for implementation of Balanced Score Card approach across the organization. Altech Star implemented the Balanced Score card using its in-house product "Business Dashboard" (now Value Insight) and successfully covered manufacturing, supply chain and sales functions.

The benefits arising out of the implementation of Business Dashboard for the manufacturing and supply chain functions were:

  1. Monitoring Downtime with respect to various parameters
  2. Monitor raw materials procurement/distribution and utilization
  3. Distributed stock management
  4. Inventory turns- Planned vs. Actual
  5. Average Inventory
  6. Ageing analysis
  7. Trend and cause analysis on defects
  8. Comparative study across different categories
  9. Pre-set alerts to alert functional managers whenever preset parameters crossed threshold limits.
