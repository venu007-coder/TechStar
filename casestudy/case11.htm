The Client: A world class Group of Hospitals having their facilities in all major cities in India, Middle East and Srilanka.

The Problem: There was a need at the Chennai facility to network their Endoscopy stations since this was a department where many medical experts conducted procedures concurrently. They wanted all images and patient details in a central server from where Individual patient reports could be generated. The challenge here was to handle stream images without getting the quality distorted.

Solution: One workstation was designated as a file server and the other workstations as clients. Among this group, any client could be used as a capturing or viewing station. The capturing station and the servers were regular workstations with a high-end configuration. The procedures done at the capturing stations were transferred to the central server at pre-determined intervals. The viewing stations could then view the transferred images.

In this configuration, one or more of the stations could be used as report preparing stations where the diagnosis reports could be prepared.
