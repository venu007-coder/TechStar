Client: A leading player in Consumer Durables in India.

The Problem: The company was struggling with Supply Chain issues due to lack of insights into huge data coming from different operational systems. The challenge was to build a Supply Chain solution to provide information to managers.

The customer wanted to have insights into:

  1. The Days of Inventory, order fill rates and inventory turns
  2. Logistics requirements
  3. Key Performance Indicators for ‘Deliver’

Solution: Altech Star conducted a study on the customer's Supply Chain information requirements, current processes and prepared a business case for a Supply Chain Business Intelligence solution based on their proprietary ‘Hyper Cube’ technology. Subsequent to the study, the Altech team worked on the ETL process and extracted the operational data from varied data sources like J.D.Edwards, AS400 application and excel data sources. The data was aggregated and exported into the multi-dimensional database for analytical reports from the ‘Hyper Cube’.

Benefits: The tool helped in tracking key Supply Chain indicators and resulted in significant improvements
