Client: Amsouth Bank, Birmingham, Alabama

Altech Star was associated with Amsouth Bank to develop a web based application to assist the credit administrators at Amsouth Bank analyze, predict and report the behavioral pattern of a set of commitments within the context of its associated risk ratings.

The application allowed the user to define migration sets - a group of commitments filtered using one or more combination of qualifiers. By deploying the solution, Amsouth Bank was able to predict the nature of behavior of a commitment (loan repayment) pertaining to a particular organisation / industry / location.
