Introduction

Welcome to PowerWebBuilder. To start building your new web site, follow the easy 6-step Wizard by clicking the Next button.

Not sure how to design your new web site? Take a look at our helpful Getting Started guide.

A few things to remember:

  1. Click the Help button for online help if you have any questions throughout the Wizard.

  2. The Next button will take you to the next step in the Wizard and save your information as you go. You can go back through the Wizard at anytime and make changes to your site as long as you have not published your site in Step 6.

  3. Once you have published your site in Step 6, your site will be available for viewing on the Internet. Any changes you would like to make to your web site after publishing can be made through the PowerWebBuilder Editor. In the Editor, you will have the chance to further customize your web site and give it that one-of-a-kind look.
