Site Information

The information on this page is optional; however, completing the fields can be helpful in driving traffic to your web site by search engines. Note - registering for search engines is not completed in this step and will have to be done separately.

Keywords:
How would you locate your web site? Pick words that would help your customers find your site. For example, product types, manufacturer names, etc. List up to 12 or so.
Site Name:
Keep the title under 64 characters. Consider using some of your keywords in the title of your home page.
Description:
Create a short paragraph that describes your site that could be displayed by search engines. Use some of your keywords within the text and keep it short (under 200 characters) but complete.
